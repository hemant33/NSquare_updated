Pharmaceuticals

Pharmaceuticals industry being one of the most process and regulatory driven industries, so it becomes imperative to have a comprehensive CRM systems that marks all the nine yards of functioning. Dynamics CRM allows capturing this vitals with highly customizable attributes that can fit into pharma environments. NSquare understand these requisites in detail and has expertise to implement end to end as well as scalable system to address Pharma industry challenges.

A single Platform to Manage functioning

  • Marketing Automation
  • Sales team tracking
  • Samples and Field Appointment
  • Defect Management
  • Analytics and KPIs
  • Dashboards

Benefits

  • A common platform for maintaining a constant and strong relationship with customers (doctors and patients)
  • Improvement in workforce productivity
  • Capturing and utilizing data
  • A combine disparate contact networks into a single unified database
  • A stable centralized system

Why Dynamics 365 is the suitable solution

  • It is possible to maintain customizable price and discount lists for all your business needs with dynamics 365. This allows your sales representatives to create quotes, invoices and orders all from Dynamics 365.
  • Extensive reporting, analytics of data and visual representation to the representatives is possible.
  • Ensures that queries, cases and general responses to customers are fast tracked and tackled immediately using assignment rules and alerts.
  • Campaign management.
  • Offer cloud–based databases, giving personnel access to all the information they need. When coupled with mobile integration, this helps representatives on mobile devices provide real–time information on sales to the home office immediately.
